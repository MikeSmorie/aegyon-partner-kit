Primary Structural Thesis

Why This Thesis Matters

If this thesis holds, Salesforce faces simultaneous margin compression and growth deceleration as enterprise customers gain procurement leverage through AI-powered competitive benchmarking while simultaneously evaluating lower-cost AI-native alternatives. This dual force accelerates renewal churn risk, reduces net revenue retention, and compresses operating leverage—potentially forcing capital structure decisions, strategic M&A, or material cost reductions within 12–24 months. The consequence is not immediate insolvency but strategic constraint: reduced optionality, increased cost of capital, and potential valuation multiple compression that limits access to growth financing.
