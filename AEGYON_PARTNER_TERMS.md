## PARTNERSHIP STRUCTURE

Consultants, advisors, and service providers can embed AEGYON structural diagnostics into their client offerings through a simple **50/50 revenue share model**.

### How It Works

**Partner:** Manages client relationship, handles sales, coordinates delivery, collects payment  
**AEGYON:** Provides analysis methodology, delivers dossier + dashboard, supports execution

**Revenue Split:** Partner keeps 50%, pays AEGYON 50% of invoice total

---

## ENGAGEMENT FLOW

### 1. PARTNER SELLS TO CLIENT
- Partner proposes AEGYON structural assessment to their client
- Partner quotes price (see pricing tiers below)
- Partner collects payment from client
- Partner notifies AEGYON of engagement

### 2. AEGYON DELIVERS
- AEGYON receives engagement details from partner (company info, focus areas, timeline)
- AEGYON completes structural dossier + interactive dashboard per timeline
- AEGYON delivers to partner; partner delivers to end client
- AEGYON available for client methodology questions (optional direct communication)

### 3. PAYMENT & RECONCILIATION
- Partner invoices client for full assessment fee (e.g., $20K)
- Partner remits 50% to AEGYON ($10K) within 15 days of invoice payment
- Partner retains 50% ($10K)

- Partner keeps: $10,000 – $25,000

**Annual Retainer: $100,000 – $250,000/year**

- Partner quotes retainer to client
- Partner keeps 50% of retainer fee

## WHAT'S INCLUDED IN ASSESSMENT

All tiers include:

1. **Structural Dossier (PDF)**
   - Executive summary
   - 10-domain assessment (capital, regulatory, market, competitive, operational, customer, tech, talent, synergy, governance)
   - Forcing events & decision timelines
   - Recommendations & risk escalation protocol

2. **Interactive Dashboard (HTML)**
   - Tabbed interface with domain details
   - Risk RAG table (Red/Amber/Green)
   - Governance assessment matrix
   - Decision cornering matrix (Tier 2+)
   - Thesis invalidation watch (Tier 3)

3. **Supporting Materials**
   - Data gap register (what else would help)
   - Stress testing assumptions
   - Competitive positioning (Tier 2+)
   - 5-year scenario analysis (Tier 3)

## EXCLUSIONS (WHAT'S NOT INCLUDED)

- ❌ Operational due diligence (we don't do ops walk-throughs)
- ❌ Financial modeling or detailed accounting analysis
- ❌ Legal opinion or regulatory compliance sign-off
- ❌ Customer interviews or vendor deep dives
- ❌ Ongoing monitoring/retainer (sold separately)
- ❌ Revisions beyond scope (>2 update rounds included)

## PAYMENT TERMS & CONDITIONS

### Invoicing
1. Partner receives assessment from AEGYON
2. Partner invoices client for full amount
3. Partner receives payment from client
4. Partner remits AEGYON share within 15 days of client payment

### Late Payment
- If partner payment to AEGYON exceeds 30 days past client payment, engagement discounts apply to future work
- After 60 days, AEGYON reserves right to communicate directly with client regarding payment status

### Pricing Agreement
- Partner prices are NOT negotiable with AEGYON
- Partner may quote lower to client (absorb difference) but cannot discount AEGYON's margin
- Example: Tier 2 assessment = $8K–$20K. Partner can sell for $12K (keeping $6K), but AEGYON still receives $6K

---

## INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY & CONFIDENTIALITY

- ✅ AEGYON retains IP on methodology, frameworks, and tooling
- ✅ Partner may not resell, repackage, or white-label AEGYON work
- ✅ Partner may not use AEGYON deliverables for other clients
- ✅ Client information remains confidential between partner, client, and AEGYON
- ✅ AEGYON may use anonymized case studies for marketing (with partner/client approval)

---

## RELATIONSHIP TERMINATION

Either party may terminate partnership with **30 days written notice**.

Upon termination:
- Existing engagements complete per original terms
- No new engagements accepted
- All client relationships revert to direct AEGYON management (if client requests)

---

## LIABILITY & INDEMNIFICATION

### AEGYON Liability
- AEGYON's assessment is provided "as-is" for decision support only
- AEGYON is not liable for client decisions made based on assessment
- AEGYON does not provide legal, financial, or operational advice
- AEGYON liability limited to amount paid by partner (50% of client fee)

### Partner Liability
- Partner is responsible for client relationship management
- Partner must ensure client understands assessment scope & limitations
- Partner must clearly communicate that AEGYON assessment is one input, not definitive answer

### Mutual Indemnification
Both parties agree to indemnify each other from third-party claims arising from:
- Breach of confidentiality
- IP infringement by either party
- Fraudulent misrepresentation of services
